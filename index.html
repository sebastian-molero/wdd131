Bogotá, Colombia

Colombia is a country in northwest South America and the sole nation in the region with both Pacific Ocean and Caribbean Sea coastlines. It is among the most biodiverse nations on Earth, with extensive arrays of ecosystems. Colombia is famously renowned for its coffee, flowers, and richness in culture. It also has some major festivals, including the Carnaval de Barranquilla. The diversity of its people, music, and cuisine make Colombia a unique and lively place.
